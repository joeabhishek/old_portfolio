Familiarity Graph

Familiarity graph is a interactive data visualization that shows the degree of familiarity between a student and his college alumni. . Each dot in the visualization represents a person and the size of the dot denotes their seniority. You are the red dot. The dots in the very similar region match your preferences best and the matching reduces as you traverse outwards. The graph is fully interactive where hovering on dot displays the person's name and clicking on the dot shows all the information about that person. It features a comprehensive search functionality to apply filters dynamically on the data.

It was co-developed with Samuel Pushpak. K, a Post Graduate student from National Institute of Design, India. We have used D3.js for visualizing and PHP for processing the data.
