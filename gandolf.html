gandolf

Relocation has been become mundane nowadays, be it IT employees, students, aspiring musicians, actors, artists, the list goes on and on. People always find themselves travelling to a new place more often than not.
Buses are a common mode of transport in India. Everyday we see lakhs of people travelling to different places of the country on buses. There are thousands of Travel agencies which offer buses to almost every place in the country. Catching a bus in an unknown city to another unknown city can be a cumbersome process.
1. You might not know the location of the boarding point.
2. You might not know how long it takes for you to reach the boarding point.
3. You might be worried if the bus has already reached your boarding point.
4. You might not know if the bus is running late.
5. If you missed a bus at a particular stop, how will you know where the next stop is and when it is expected to reach that point.
gandolf   attempts to ease all these pain points and also help you explore the new city in an intuituve way.

As you can see in the first screenshot above, you can track your bus stop by stop. The solid green line indicates that the bus has already travelled to the stop. The dotted green line indicates that bus is on the way to the next stop. The gray line indicates that the bus is yet to travel the path. The card below the tracker card gives the route to your boarding point with the distance and duration details.
Once you've reached your destination and settled down, you might want to explore the new city. gandolf   helps you do that as well. When you click on the explore page from the side menu, you get an explorer like the second screenshot above. You can enter any place you would like to visit and the app will display the route, distance and duration details. Click on the places nearyby card to open a more suggestions.

The Places Nearby page has many categories you can choose from. From restaurants to banking to shopping, covers it all. There's a small card below which suggests places from different categories based on your previous searches.
Once you click on a particular category, the app navigates to a page with suggestions for that particular category. You can navigate to other categories in this page by clicking on the side arrows at the top of the card.

gandolf   basically tries to address every pain point a traveller might face in a new city. It tries to give you a bigger picture of the places you travel to. It tries to make show you everything a new city has to offer and help you get connect with the culture. All in all, gandolf   tries to give you the confidence of a local when you travel.
