NFold - RNA Structure Visualization and Prediction

NFold is an RNA secondary structure predication and visualization tool. Biologists use RNA structure for various studies and therefore visualizing the large and complex RNA strands becomes very vital. NFold's structure prediction is based on an enhanced version of Nussinov's algorithm where in addition to Watson-Crick pairs, G-U wobble pairs are also allowed. Another important modification is made in terms of scoring the bond pairs, instead of scoring all the bond pairs with 1, C-G bond is given the highest score of 3, followed by A-U with 2 and G-U pair is scored with 1. In spite of this overhead, the enhanced algorithmâ€™s traceback phase is still linear in time and memory. The predicted structures are visualized into, bracketed, planar graph and matrix representations. Python is used at the backend to analyze and process large RNA strings and to implement the enhanced Nussinov's algorithm. The representations are rendered in SVG using JavaScript
